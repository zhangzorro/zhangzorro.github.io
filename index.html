I am currently an Assistant Professor at the Department of Management Science, School of Management, Xiamen University. I obtained my BS in E-Commerce from Xiamen University in 2016, MS in Business Analytics and PhD in Operations Management from The Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2017 and 2022 respectively. Prior to joining XMU in 2023, I was a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the Department of Decisions, Operations, and Technology of CUHK Business School.

My research interests include Pricing and Revenue Management, Data-driven Decision Making, and Sustainable Operations.
